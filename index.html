Overview

We have created an app for the new UNC Golf Practice Facility. The app is being designed to serve anyone who uses the new practice facility including guests, team members, alumni, and coaches.

We hope that this app will gamify practice at the facility and increase competition between players which will not only drive more practice but will also increase focus.

App Sections

Dynamic Map

Gives users an overview of the facility, and provides measurement capabilities.

Drills

A list of drills with the ability to record performance and automatically send push notifications when completed.

Leaderboard

Provides a place for everyone to compete and lists player's performance on drills.

Admin

Will allow coaches to gauge players performance throughout the facility and manage users on the app.
