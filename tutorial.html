Tutorial

Download The App

  1. Navigate to the iOS App Store
  2. Search for "UNC Golf"
  3. Follow the standard procedures for installing an ios app
+

Create Your Account

  1. Open the app and click "sign up"
  2. Fill in requested fields. You must use an email address that has not already been used
  3. Wait for a coach to approve your app access
+

Start a Drill

  1. Navigate to the "Drills" tab at the bottom
  2. Use the filter options to find a desired drill
  3. Once selceted, review the drill steps and overview
  4. After completition, add notes and your score and click "log"
+

View Team's Performance

  1. Click the "activity" tab at the bottom of the screen
  2. Scroll and view the teams peformance
  3. Click the "Attempt Drill" button if you wish to compete
+

Delete Your Account

  1. Navigate to the "Dashboard" tab
  2. Click the profile icon in the top right
  3. Click delete account and confirm deletion on the following prompt
  4. Your account and it's linked data are now removed
+

Report a performance reocord or notification

  1. Navigate to the "Activity" tab
  2. Find the activity you wish to report
  3. Swipe left on the activity and click report
  4. We will review your report
